# Account Dashboard

After you log in to Squarespace with your credentials, you'll arrive at your Account Dashboard. This is your starting point for managing your website and account settings.

## What Can You Do Here?

From the Account Dashboard, you can:

- View and manage your account settings
- Access your website details
- Create new websites
- Manage your domains

## Account Settings

Click the **menu icon** in the top right corner to access your account settings and support options.

### Profile

Here you can update your profile picture and personal information associated with your account.

### Security

The Security section helps you protect your account with several features:

- **Two-Factor Authentication** — Add extra security to your login
- **Password Management** — Change your email and password
- **App Passwords** — Create special passwords for devices that don't support two-factor authentication
- **Connected Apps** — View external apps (apps not made by Squarespace) that are linked to your website

### Activity

The Activity section shows you:

- Where your account is currently logged in from
- Your website domains
- How many websites are associated with your account
- Settings and permission details

### Email Notifications

You can control which emails Squarespace sends you:

- Partner emails and product announcements appear as green checkboxes
- Check the box to receive an email, or uncheck it to stop receiving those emails
- You can unsubscribe from individual notifications or all at once
- By default, Squarespace will notify you when someone leaves a comment on your website

### Language

Choose your preferred language for using Squarespace.

## Getting Help and Logging Out

At the bottom left of the Account Settings menu, you'll find quick links to:

- The **Help Center** — for additional support
- **Log Out** — to safely exit your account

## Next: Website Dashboard

Once you've reviewed your account settings, you're ready to explore your website. Your websites are listed on the main dashboard—click on your website to access the Website Dashboard.

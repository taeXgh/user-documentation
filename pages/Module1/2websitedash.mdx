# Website Dashboard

The Website Dashboard is your main workspace for managing and editing your website. When you first enter, you'll see a preview of your website and a summary of its performance.

## Main Sections

The Website Dashboard has four main areas you'll use:

1. **Website** — Edit your site content
2. **Contacts** — View form submissions
3. **Analytics** — Track visitor activity
4. **Settings** — Configure website options

## Website Menu

### Editing Your Site

Click the **Edit** button in the top left to enter editing mode. Here you can:

- Change images and text
- Add or modify links
- Update sections of your website

When you hover over any section, it will be highlighted. Click the **Edit Section** button (top right of the section) to change how that section looks. To edit text or images, double-click on the element.

### Viewing Your Site

- **Play Button** — Preview how your website looks to visitors
- **Mobile View Button** — See how your site appears on phones and tablets
- **Site Styles Button** — Edit the color scheme and theme

### Pages Tab

View all pages of your website in the left panel. Click the gear icon next to any page to access:

- General settings
- Navigation settings
- SEO settings
- Social image settings
- Advanced settings

### Site Styles

Customize the look of your entire website:

- Change the color scheme and theme
- Adjust fonts, colors, and buttons
- Modify forms and other elements
- Add animations and adjust spacing

### Assets

All images and files used on your website are stored in one place:

- Click **Add Folder** to organize your assets
- Click **Upload** to add new images or files
- Reuse assets across multiple pages

### SEO and AI Visibility

Squarespace's Beacon AI helps improve your website's search engine visibility:

- Check your current SEO score
- Review recommendations for improvement
- Access SEO settings from the settings menu
- Watch helpful guides to learn more

## Contacts Menu

View all form submissions from visitors:

- See who filled out forms on your website
- Use the **Filter** button to search by form type or other details
- Review and respond to inquiries

## Analytics Menu

Track how visitors interact with your website:

### Traffic

- View a graph of your website visits
- See what devices and browsers visitors use
- Check operating systems and device types

### Search Keywords

- See which search terms bring visitors to your site
- Understand what keywords are working for you

### Geography

- View where in the world your visitors are located

### Engagement

- Check how many times each page is viewed
- See how much time visitors spend on each page
- Review your activity log for detailed visitor information

### Form and Button Conversions

- Track form submissions
- See how many visitors click on buttons

# Settings Menu

The Settings Menu contains all the options for configuring your website and account. Here's what you can do in each section.

## Website Settings

### Suitability

Control who can access your website:

- **Private** — Only people you invite can see it
- **Public** — Anyone can find and view it
- **Password Protected** — Visitors must enter a password to view it

### Site Languages

Choose the default language for your website.

### Regional Settings

Set your:

- Location and time zone
- How measurements are displayed (metric or imperial)

### Cookies and Data Privacy

Add a banner to your website asking visitors about their cookie preferences:

- Currently disabled by default
- Click to enable the cookie consent banner

### Favicon Settings

Edit the small icon that appears in the browser tab:

- Edit the current favicon
- Replace it with a new image

### Social Links

Add links to your social media accounts:

- Display them on your website using the editor
- Connect your social media presence to your site

### Site Promotion

Allow Squarespace to help promote your website.

### Image Settings

Optimize how images load and display on your website (currently enabled).

## Domains and Emails

- **Register a new domain** — Buy a domain name through Squarespace
- **Manage existing domains** — Update domain information
- **Google Workspace** — Set up a professional email address through Google's business tools

## Marketing

Configure settings to help promote your website:

- **SEO Settings** — Optimize your site for search engines
- **Meta Pixel ID** — Connect to Facebook/Meta to track visitors and target ads

## Third-Party Tools

### Integrations

View and manage:

- Connected accounts from other services
- Apps linked to your website

### Extensions

Add Squarespace extensions to expand your website's features:

- Click **Extensions** to browse available add-ons
- Expand your website's capabilities

## Permissions and Ownership

- **Invite People** — Allow others to edit your website
- **Manage Permissions** — Control what each team member can do
- **Transfer Ownership** — Give someone else full control of the website

## Billing

Manage your account finances:

- **Payment Method** — Add or update your credit card or payment information
- **Subscription** — View and manage your Squarespace plan
- **Invoices** — View your billing history and receipts

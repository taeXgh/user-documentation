# Squarespace Help Features

Squarespace offers multiple resources to help you learn and get support when you need it.

## Help Center

Squarespace has a comprehensive Help Center with information on almost every topic. Key features include:

### Live Chat Support

- A chatbot appears in the bottom right corner
- The chatbot can answer common questions
- Connect with a live agent for more complex issues

### Video Tutorials

- Link to the Squarespace Help YouTube channel
- Browse playlists organized by topic
- Learn about editing, design, marketing, and more

### Additional Resources

Scroll to the bottom of the Help Center to find:

- **Webinars** — Live training sessions
- **Forums** — Community discussions and advice
- **Marketplace** — Browse experts, templates, and extensions

## Marketplace and Expert Help

### Browse Experts

Connect with professional website designers and Squarespace experts:

- Find experts matched to your needs
- Review their profiles and pricing
- Hire someone to help you:
  - Polish and improve your existing site
  - Build a brand new website
  - Add specific features or functionality

## When You Need Help

If you're stuck or unsure about something:

1. Check the **Help Center** for quick answers
2. Try the **Live Chat** on the Help Center
3. Watch a **tutorial video** on the YouTube channel
4. Ask a question in the **community forums**
5. **Hire an expert** from the marketplace for professional assistance

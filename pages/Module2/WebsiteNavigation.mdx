# Website Navigation

This guide explains how visitors navigate through the ATCAP website and what they'll find on each page.

## Home Page

The landing page visitors see first includes:

### Main Sections

- **About Us** — Link to learn more about ATCAP
- **Services** — Buttons linking to each service page
- **Contact Us** — Link to the contact form

### Footer

Apears on every page and includes:

- Links to all website pages
- Office location and address
- Hours of operation
- Contact methods (phone, email)

## About Us Page

Provides information about ATCAP including:

- Company mission
- Company values
- Background and history

## Services Pages

Each service has its own dedicated page that explains:

- What the service includes
- Who the service is for
- How to get started with that service

Visitors can click on service cards from the home page to learn more about each service.

## Contact Us Page

Visitors can interact with ATCAP through:

### Contact Form

Visitors fill out:

- First and last name
- Email address
- Message or inquiry

Once submitted, the message is sent to the ATCAP email for review and response.

### Location Information

- Clickable phone number to call the office
- Embedded map showing the office location

## Insurance Page

Provides information about payment options:

- Images and links to accepted health insurance providers
- Information for visitors with private pay options

## Resources Page

A comprehensive reference for visitors with links and information organized by topic:

- **Nursing and Professional Organizations** — Industry resources and associations
- **General Health and Medical Information** — Health education and medical references
- **Caregiving Support and Family Resources** — Support for family members
- **Mental Health Resources** — Mental health and counseling services
- **Senior and Aging Resources** — Resources for older adults
- **Pediatric and Child Health Resources** — Health information for children

Each category can be expanded to see detailed information and links to external resources.

## Apply Now Page

For potential applicants interested in working with ATCAP, the application form collects:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Resume
- Availability

This captures Phase 1 information for the application process.
